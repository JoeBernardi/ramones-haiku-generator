Ramones Haiku Generator

Who doesn't like the Ramones? Their oft-overlooked lyrics (written largely by Dee Dee, strangely) lend themselves really well to cut-and-paste stuff like this. Haiku don't have to be 5-7-5 (I think?), so the generator's normal mode just grabs three random lines from the first three Ramones albums. For purists, though, its syllable-counting mode creates a haiku by starting with a random word from those albums and counting forward until it reaches the correct number of syllables. (If it goes over, it just starts again.)
